Humanities

Humanities is a class taught by Ms. Small. In this class we essentially learn the history of the world through literature. This prompts us to read texts from various authors of different time periods and cultures. After we read the assigned texts for homework, we discuss it as a class in our sections the next day. Once all discussions are through, we are assigned an essay on broad topics, which allows for a lot of freedom. We get about a week for each essay, with an opportunity to rewrite it with corrections made.
